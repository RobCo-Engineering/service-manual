## Pip-Boy 3000 Mk V <a name="pip-boy-3000-mk-v"></a>

The following sections will guide you through creating apps and games for the
Pip-Boy 3000 Mk V.

### Repositories <a name="repositories"></a>

Most knowledge about creating apps for the Pip-Boy 3000 Mk V can be found in the
following repositories:

- https://github.com/thewandcompany/pip-boy

  The official repository for the core/default Pip-Boy 3000 Mk V firmware and
  apps.

- https://github.com/CodyTolene/pip-boy-apps

  The fan made repository for apps hosted on [Pip-Boy.com][link-pip-boy-com].

- https://github.com/CodyTolene/pip-apps

  The first apps repo. Archived after TWC released their tool. May stay hold
  reference value.
